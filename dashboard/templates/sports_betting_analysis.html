Strategy Overview

Optimal ROI Strategy: Based on historical analysis of 2,885+ NBA games, betting on the trailing team when the halftime lead is between 15-17 points maximizes return on investment at +$17.31 per $100 wagered.

Key Insights:
  • SKIP 12-14pt - Win rate near breakeven, negative expected value
  • BET 14-15pt - Profitable, but not optimal
  • OPTIMAL 15-17pt - Best ROI with +9.1% edge
  • BET 17-20pt - Profitable, but diminishing game frequency

This strategy exploits "garbage time regression" where winning teams rest starters and trailing teams play aggressively in the second half.
