The scanner identifies pairs of Polymarket markets that have logical relationships but are priced independently. When the combined probabilities violate logical constraints (e.g., mutually exclusive events summing to >100%), it identifies arbitrage opportunities.

Example Scenario

Mutually Exclusive Mispricing:

Market A: "Candidate X wins election" = 55%
Market B: "Candidate Y wins election" = 52%

Combined: 107% (impossible!)
Opportunity: Short both, guaranteed 7% profit
